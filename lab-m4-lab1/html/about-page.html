Meet the developer behind Brew Review

Manuel Sandoz Santiago is a 26 year old computer engineer student currently going through his last semester of college. He was born and raised on Puerto Rico where he lived until recently. Manuel is passionate about programming, basketball, and coffee. It is his passion for coffee that inspired the concep of Brew Review a page where coffee lovers can go and share their thoughts on their most recent and beloved coffee spots. The idea is to help people who like coffee find new places to visit and expand their knowledge of coffee. On his free time Manuel can be found programming, enjoying a book over a cup of coffee or hiking.
